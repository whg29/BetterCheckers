Better Checkers

Team Members: Rahul Patel, Kang, Walter Gottlieb,In Sung Park

Checkers is a board game played by two persons on a board of 64 light and dark squares, similar to a chessboard. Better Checkers is a web-based game, written in the Java programming language, that allows two players to play checkers. Players simply need to access the web page and then they may proceed with playing the game.
